Italian

The Bay Area houses many delicious Italian restaurants, with incredible atmospheres, amazing service, and authentic cuisine.

  1. Little Original Joe's
  2. Cucina sa
  3. Delarosa
  4. Roma Antica

Little Original Joe's

This classic yet modernized Italian restaurant has the perfect ambiance for just a casual lunch or girls' night out. The rich pasta sauces and unique appetizers enhance the amazing experience, along with the great service. I definitely recommend this hotspot, and while you do need 8 people to make a reservation, it is definitely worth it.

Cucina sa

Cucina San Anslelmo has comforting food, amazing service, and great vibes. Their menu has countless delicious appetizers and rich main courses. The location, in the center of downtown San Anselmo, is a wondeful spot to meet new people, eat great food, and wander down to the San Anselmo creek.
